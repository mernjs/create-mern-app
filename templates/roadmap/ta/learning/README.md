# 1. Technical Leadership

● **Cross-Team Collaboration** – Drive seamless collaboration across Engineering, AI/ML, DevOps, QA, Product, UI/UX, Data Engineering, and Business teams to ensure alignment on technical strategy, delivery goals, architectural standards, and business objectives.

● **Technical Vision & Roadmap** – Define and communicate long-term technology vision, architecture roadmaps, and modernization strategies aligned with organizational goals and future business growth.

● **Architecture Governance** – Establish architecture standards, governance processes, review mechanisms, and technical decision frameworks to maintain consistency, scalability, and quality across solutions.

● **Stakeholder Communication** – Effectively communicate architectural decisions, technical trade-offs, risks, mitigation strategies, and recommendations to both technical and business stakeholders.

● **Technical Decision Making** – Evaluate technologies, platforms, frameworks, and architectural approaches to recommend optimal solutions based on business, technical, and operational requirements.

● **Technology Standardization** – Define and govern approved frameworks, libraries, cloud services, development methodologies, and engineering best practices across the organization.

● **Technical Debt Management** – Continuously identify, prioritize, and reduce technical debt while balancing innovation, delivery timelines, and long-term maintainability.

# 2. Pre-Sales & Solutioning

● **Client Requirement Gathering** – Conduct discovery workshops, stakeholder discussions, and requirement analysis sessions to understand business objectives, challenges, constraints, and success criteria.

● **Client Calls & Technical Discussions** – Lead technical discussions, solution workshops, architecture reviews, and stakeholder meetings to align expectations and technical direction.

● **Business Problem Analysis** – Analyze complex business challenges and translate them into scalable, secure, and maintainable technology solutions.

● **Solution Proposal Creation** – Develop comprehensive solution proposals, architecture blueprints, implementation strategies, and technology recommendations tailored to client requirements.

● **High-Level Architecture Design** – Define scalable system architectures, integration approaches, deployment strategies, and technical frameworks to support business goals.

● **Effort Estimation** – Prepare detailed estimations covering development effort, timelines, infrastructure requirements, operational costs, and resource planning.

● **Risk & Feasibility Assessment** – Evaluate technical feasibility, identify project risks, dependencies, assumptions, and define mitigation strategies.

● **Proof of Concepts (POC)** – Design and develop proof-of-concepts and prototypes to validate technical assumptions and demonstrate solution feasibility.

● **Technical Presentations & Demos** – Deliver compelling technical presentations, architecture walkthroughs, and solution demonstrations to business and technical audiences.

● **RFP/RFI Support** – Contribute to technical proposals by preparing architecture sections, implementation strategies, solution approaches, and cost estimations.

● **Cloud Cost Estimation** – Analyze and forecast infrastructure, AI model consumption, storage, networking, and operational costs to support solution planning.

● **AI Solution Consulting** – Recommend suitable AI, ML, LLM, RAG, Agentic AI, and automation solutions based on business requirements and expected outcomes.

● **Competitive Analysis** – Evaluate competing technologies, platforms, and vendor offerings to recommend the most effective and future-ready solutions.

● **Design Gap Analysis** – Review proposed designs against business needs, technical feasibility, scalability requirements, and implementation constraints.

● **System Architecture Design** – Design enterprise-grade architectures that prioritize scalability, reliability, security, maintainability, and long-term extensibility.

● **Data Architecture** – Design databases, schemas, data models, storage strategies, and data flow architectures to support operational and analytical workloads.

● **Architecture Documentation** – Create and maintain HLDs, LLDs, architecture decision records, technical specifications, and implementation guidelines.

● **Diagram Design** – Produce comprehensive architecture artifacts including System Diagrams, DFDs, UMLs, ERDs, Sequence Diagrams, Deployment Diagrams, and Infrastructure Diagrams.

● **API Design & Documentation** – Define API standards, contracts, authentication strategies, versioning approaches, and documentation practices for scalable integrations.

● **Integration Architecture** – Design secure and scalable integrations with internal systems, third-party platforms, cloud services, and enterprise applications.

● **Non-Functional Requirements Mapping** – Ensure architecture incorporates performance, security, scalability, reliability, availability, maintainability, and compliance requirements.

# 6. Artificial Intelligence & Generative AI Architecture

● **LLM Solution Design** – Architect enterprise-grade LLM-powered applications that integrate business workflows, domain knowledge, and intelligent automation capabilities.

● **RAG Architecture** – Design Retrieval-Augmented Generation architectures leveraging vector databases, enterprise knowledge repositories, and intelligent retrieval mechanisms.

● **Agentic AI Architecture** – Design and implement autonomous multi-agent systems capable of reasoning, orchestration, task execution, and workflow automation.

● **Prompt Engineering Standards** – Establish prompt engineering methodologies, optimization practices, evaluation frameworks, and governance standards.

● **Model Selection Strategy** – Evaluate OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Llama, Mistral, and other models based on performance, cost, security, and business requirements.

● **Vector Database Architecture** – Design scalable vector search solutions using Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Chroma, OpenSearch, and related technologies.

● **AI Evaluation Frameworks** – Define frameworks for evaluating model accuracy, response quality, hallucination rates, latency, and business effectiveness.

● **AI Security & Guardrails** – Implement AI safety mechanisms including prompt protection, content moderation, policy enforcement, privacy controls, and guardrails.

● **LLMOps Architecture** – Design end-to-end LLMOps pipelines covering deployment, monitoring, evaluation, versioning, experimentation, and governance.

● **AI Cost Optimization** – Optimize model usage, token consumption, infrastructure utilization, and inference costs while maintaining performance.

● **Fine-Tuning Strategy** – Evaluate and implement model customization, fine-tuning, domain adaptation, and continual learning approaches.
