# No-Code & Low-Code Learning & Interview Hub

A comprehensive knowledge repository focused on No-Code and Low-Code platforms, business process automation, workflow orchestration, rapid application development, SaaS creation, integrations, and digital transformation.

This module is designed for Business Analysts, Product Managers, Founders, Automation Specialists, Citizen Developers, Solution Architects, Technical Architects, and Technology Leaders who want to build scalable business solutions without extensive coding while understanding enterprise-grade architecture and automation practices.

---

# Objectives

This module aims to:

- Understand No-Code and Low-Code ecosystems
- Build applications without traditional programming
- Automate business processes and workflows
- Create MVPs and production-ready applications rapidly
- Integrate multiple systems and platforms
- Design scalable No-Code architectures
- Implement business automation solutions
- Understand governance and security best practices
- Evaluate No-Code platform capabilities and limitations
- Prepare for No-Code, Automation, and Solution Architecture discussions
